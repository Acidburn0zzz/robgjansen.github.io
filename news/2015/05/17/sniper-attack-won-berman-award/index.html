The Sniper Attack Won the NRL Berman Award

Sunday, May 17th, 2015 | Comments

Our paper entitled “The Sniper Attack: Anonymously Deanonymizing and Disabling the Tor Network” won the 47th Annual Alan Berman Research Publication Award! This award was given by the U.S. Naval Research Laboratory on 2015-04-17 for best paper in the Information Technology Division.
